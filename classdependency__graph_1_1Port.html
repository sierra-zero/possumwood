void dependency_graph::Port::linkTo ( Port &  targetPort)

"links" two ports - dirtiness and values of source (this) port are directly transferred to target port. Usable primarily for subnetworks, but might have other uses as well (e.g., "referencing"). Can effectively replace "compute". Only output ports can be linked.
